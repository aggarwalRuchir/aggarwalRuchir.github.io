About Me

I am a computer engineer based in Ann Arbor, MI. I recently completed my Masters degree from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I am interested in working on literally anything but primarily my work involves use of AI related technologies and frameworks, and some of my work involves system designing and software development. I love the prospect of human like intelligence supplementing peoples' everyday lives and want to develop products that do exactly that.
